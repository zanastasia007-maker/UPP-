# Ritual 1: Invite the Stick

> "Bring the measuring rod. Let truth be seen."

## When to use
Before any decision affecting +10 lives.

## Steps
1. **Pause** – 30 seconds of review 
2. **Name the Stake** – "Who wins? Who loses?"
3. **Hold the Stick** – One person speaks truth without interruption
4. **Mark the Wall** – Write outcome on visible surface
5. **Resume only if all nod**

## Activation Phrase
> “I invite the stick.”
